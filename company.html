Meaning of Dhi

Dhi (Sanskrit) is pronounced as “Thee – as in Thee and Thou” – and signifies “intelligence” which translates the company name to “Intelligent Insights”. Dhi is the essence of what the company is trying to bring our customers by pushing beyond visualization into the world of multi-dimensional analytics

History

Dhi Insights is majority owned by InRhytm, Inc. a 15 year old data analytics firm.

,

Mission

“To provide data driven insights that help drive performance using AI tools”

Visualization is the first step on the journey of data analysis. The multi-dimensional nature of business drivers makes it difficult for even seasoned analysts to mine insights from large databases. Our vision is to use technology to automate the process behind analytics and to provide the “story” behind key insights. Our mission is to not make you a data scientist but to provide you with a virtual assistant to help your journey of discovering insights.

Values

  • We operate on the core principles of honesty, integrity, and respect for our customers, employees, and stakeholders.
  • We understand that the reason we exist is to create value for our customers.
  • We realize that innovation and disruptive thinking needs to be part of our employee ethos.
