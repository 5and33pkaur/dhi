Why Dhi

Instant Text Summarization. Not Everybody has Time to Become an Analyst

Dhi Analyst enables you to infer insights from ANY report format and summarize the key findings in written text. Using a powerful semantic layer along with NLP/NLG, users are provided with meaningful insights and summaries with minimal effort or customization.
